Your data

During launching notifications, we save your PSID assigned to your Facebook account, which is necessary to use our application. We get your PSID in moment of sending a unique code to our Facebook Messenger Bot, which you got after send wanted cosmetic items. Your data is used only for items notifications purposes and will not be shared with anyone.
You are able to remove your PSID by turning notifications off. After stop, your PSID is erased from our database.
